Scientific discussions are always great. Scientific discussions in pâtisseries are even better. The idea of the eclairs has been born during a lazy and indulgent Saturday morning. With my favourite mathematician, we went to taste some delicious chocolate eclairs and we had a long chat about optimization and lanthanides. We enjoyed so much those eclairs, the best in the world in our opinion, that we decided to replicate the experience a few weeks later, this time picking another topic to discuss. With time, we created a list of favourite topics to talk every time we crave for that very special eclair.

I have decided to reinterpret some of those discussions in the form of dialogues between a curious monkey and a wise elephant. I let you decide if I am more monkey or elephant.
